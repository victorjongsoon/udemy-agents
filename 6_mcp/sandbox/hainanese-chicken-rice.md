# Hainanese Chicken Rice Recipe

## Ingredients

### For the Chicken:
- 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 lbs)
- 4-5 slices of ginger
- 4-5 stalks of green onions
- Salt

### For the Rice:
- 2 cups jasmine rice
- 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, minced
- 2 tbsp chicken fat or vegetable oil
- 3 cups chicken broth (from boiling the chicken)

### For the Chili Sauce:
- 3 red chilies, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tsp sugar
- Salt to taste

### For the Ginger Sauce:
- 3 tbsp grated ginger
- 2-3 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt to taste

### For the Soy Sauce Mixture:
- 4 tbsp light soy sauce
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp chicken broth

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the Chicken:**
   - Clean the chicken thoroughly and rub it with salt to exfoliate.
   - Stuff the chicken cavity with ginger slices and green onions.
   - Boil a large pot of water, then immerse the chicken and cook for about 40-50 minutes until fully cooked.
   - Immediately plunge the cooked chicken into ice water to stop cooking and tighten the skin.
   - Set aside.

2. **Prepare the Rice:**
   - Rinse the jasmine rice until water runs clear and drain.
   - In a pan, heat chicken fat or vegetable oil, saut√© garlic and ginger until fragrant.
   - Add the drained rice and fry for a few minutes to coat and toast.
   - Transfer the rice to a rice cooker, add chicken broth, and cook until done.

3. **Make the Chili Sauce:**
   - Blend red chilies, garlic, ginger, lime juice, sugar, and salt until smooth.

4. **Make the Ginger Sauce:**
   - Heat vegetable oil and pour over grated ginger and salt. Mix well.

5. **Prepare the Soy Sauce Mixture:**
   - Mix light soy sauce, sesame oil, and chicken broth.

6. **Serve:**
   - Chop the chicken into serving pieces.
   - Serve chicken with fragrant rice, chili sauce, ginger sauce, soy sauce mixture, and cucumber slices on the side.

Enjoy your delicious and authentic Hainanese Chicken Rice!
